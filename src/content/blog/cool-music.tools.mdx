## Notation

###Find the chord you played 

Do you ever find yourself in a situation where you had a nice jam and played some chords but did not know the name of them? Here is the solution:

- [oolimuo](https://www.oolimo.com/en/guitar-chords/analyze) helps to know what chord you played during that epic jam
  - [oolimuo fretboard](https://www.oolimo.com/en/chord-types/sus-add)
- (scaleschords)[https://www.scales-chords.com/chordid.php]

### writing down song ideas

- [chord chart studio](https://chord-chart-studio.netlify.app/app)
